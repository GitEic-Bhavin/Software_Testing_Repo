Project Planning
---

1. Developer - Help to build applications
2. Tester - Will test
3. Designer - Design the applications
4. Supporter - Will support project once the project has gone live
5. Business Analyst - Will meet the customer and understand its requirements for product and projects. Making sure those all requirements are well documented.
6. Project Manager - Make sure that the project which we are going to deliver is of a good quality and its delivered to the customer on time or before time.

What does project management for project ?
---

**Project Manager** - will ensure for resource manageement, Time management, Budget management.

  **Resource Management** - Project manager will plan for how much manual resource will required for project.

  **Time Management** - Project management will plan the project such a way that the project will complete with good quality and we can deliver to the customer on/before time.

  - If we can't deliver to cutomer on time , cutomer will not happy and nagative impact will face to our team and org.

  **Budget Management** - How much budget is decided and in which way the budget should use and pay to employee and other things.

Best Practices of Test Manager
---

1. Build a team of test professinoals & Not testers
2. Track test executions Progress.
3. Collect simple measurement for process improvements.

    1. Defect Fix time - How much avg time to fix each defacts.
    2. Defect Test Time - How much avg time it has taken us from fiximg the defact to testing the defacts
    3. defact fixed failure rate - Percentage of fixed that fall on retest. How many time your product is failed during retest.
    4. Defact Age - Total time requires to testing product including retesting
